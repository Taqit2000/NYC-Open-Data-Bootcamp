About Us Page

This is a simple web app designed to show a heatmap of arrest data in NYC (provided by NYC Open Data) with an overlay of all subway station enterances.

The purpose of the app is to show a visualization of where crimes have happened in the past to give residents and tourists visiting New York an idea of what subway station enterances to avoid.

This was created and designed for NYC Open Data Bootcamp Project
